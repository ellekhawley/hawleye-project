Ceremony Venue

Liza and Jordan’s ceremony will take place at the historic Wisconsin Masonic Center, located at 301 Wisconsin Ave. in downtown Madison. Built between 1923 and 1925, this Classical Revival landmark features a grand auditorium with a proscenium stage, an especially meaningful setting for Liza, who grew up immersed in theater. The venue’s elegant architecture and rich history provide a timeless backdrop for their vows.

Reception Venue

Liza and Jordan’s reception will be held at the Hilton Garden Inn Madison Sun Prairie, located at 1220 South Grand Avenue, Sun Prairie, WI 53590. They chose this venue so guests could enjoy the celebration without worrying about transportation, thanks to the convenience of an attached hotel. A block of rooms will be reserved, so everyone can stay close and enjoy the weekend together. With a beautiful, spacious ballroom perfect for dinner and dancing, get ready to celebrate the night away with Liza and Jordan!
